Beginner's Guide

×
Rock, Paper, Scissors is a very simple game we all play during our childhood. Now play against the computer. You can win medals too.
Let's help you get started:
  • Choose any of the given options: Rock , Paper , Scissors .
  • Rock wins over Scissors and loses to Paper.
  • Paper wins over Rock and loses to Scissors.
  • Scissors wins over Paper and loses to Rock.
  • Click on "New Round" button at the end of each round to continue.
  • 1 win = Bronze Medal.
  • 3 win = Silver Medal.
  • 5 win = Gold Medal.
  • The player collecting all the 3 medals first, is the CHAMPION.
Ok, that's it. Phew!
